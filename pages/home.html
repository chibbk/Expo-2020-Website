About us:
  1. What is Expo?
    • A world's exposition is a large international exhibition designed to showcase achievements of
      nations. These exhibitions vary in character and are held in different parts of the world at a specific
      site for a period of time, ranging usually from three to six months.

  2. What is the purpose of this website?
    • This website aims to display some of the countries that are participating in this years Expo, currently taking place in Dubai.
